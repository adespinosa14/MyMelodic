Our Mission

Leveling the Playing Field in Music Education

From 4th grade through earning a Bachelor's of Music in Clarinet Performance at the University of Redlands, I witnessed firsthand the vast disparities in music education.

Students come from incredibly diverse backgrounds, some with years of formal training, others with significant gaps in their musical knowledge. What they learn can vary drastically, learving many without a solid "middle-ground" understanding.

My mission is to create a comprehensive resource where everyone can find what they need to grow, regardless of their starting point.
