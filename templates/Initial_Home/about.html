About Us

As MyMelodic, our main mission is to bridge the gap for music education. If a student decides to enter the music industry, it is clear from day 1 that there is a large disparity between the most experienced and the least experienced individual in the classroom. Allowing musicians to have a place that explains, shows, and guides students to be more advanced on their instrument would be a game changer. It would allow those students the opportunity to get some sort of direction that would otherwise be given by private instructors or a school, and allow those who have more resources the opportunity to progress at their current desired pace. We are modernizing music education, and with that, we will see growth like no other.
